Special Cards

Special cards add strategic twists and turns to every match. (No special effect on any first or last card played)

  • Eight

    The next player in the sequence is skipped and loses their turn.

    Play continues with any card matching the suit or value of the card.

  • Jack

    With two players: Acts as a skip card.

    With 3+ players: Reverses the direction of play.

    Play continues with any card matching the suit or value of the card.

  • Ace (except Spades)

    When played, the next player must choose a suit to change to.

    Play continues with any card matching the chosen suit.

  • Two

    When played, the next player must play an equal or higher scoring card, or add two cards and forfeit their turn.

    Play continues with the same suit as the previous card.

  • Seven of Hearts

    When played, the game ends immediately regardless of how many cards the player has left.

    Scores are calculated based on remaining cards in all players' hands.

    The player with the lowest total card score wins.

  • Joker

    This is a draw-4 card.

    When played, the next player must play an equal or higher scoring card, or adds four cards and forfeit their turn.

    Play continues with any card after joker.

  • Ace of Spades

    Can be played on any card.

    Blocks the effect of drawing two or four cards.

    Play continues with any card after ace-of-spades.
